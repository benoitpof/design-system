2017
Foundation

Plastic Odyssey founded by Simon Bernard and three partners around an expedition mission: circumnavigate three years to map plastic pollution and prototype low-tech recycling solutions.

2020
First R&D facility

Marseille lab opens. Eight engineers and material scientists begin systematic testing of shred / wash / extrude lines designed to be containerized and shipped to coastal regions.

2022
CDF prototype validated

Containerized Decentralized Factory v1 reaches 1kT/yr nominal throughput. First field deployment in Senegal validates the franchise model: local POAs train, operate, and earn from recyclate sales.

2025 · Today
4 sites operational

Network is live in Senegal, Philippines, Côte d'Ivoire and Indonesia. 1,200T processed annually. €2.4M revenue, 60% from recyclate sales. Series A closed at €2M.

2030
200 sites · target

Strategic ambition: 200 CDFs across 35 countries. 200kT plastic processed annually, 6,000 local jobs created, €100M cumulative revenue. Funded by Series B in 2027 and franchise self-financing.
